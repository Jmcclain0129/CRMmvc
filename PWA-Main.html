{ Welcome to our PWA }

If you are reading this line then you have successfully loaded our app icon on your homescreen which means your operating from our PWA

PWA stands for Progressive Web App. Soon enough all native mobile apps will be converted to a PWA and the old school native apps will go the way of the 8-track, cassette, cd and so on. It's the new generation of cutting edge technology and has a boatload of advanced features that until now were only accessible via a native mobile app. The most handy of these is of course the ability to send notifications as IM's (instant messages). Try having your current mobile responsive website pull that trick off. Basically, a PWA has all the benefits of a native IOS or Android Mobile App without having to actually pay to develop the app or host it on the native app platforms. Your customers can simply load your PWA from your website just like you just did with our PWA.

PWA: Progressive Web App

Progressive Web Applications load like regular web pages or websites but can offer the user functionality such as working offline, push notifications and device hardware access traditionally available only to native applications.
